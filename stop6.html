Location: 16011 Grant St, Thornton

Topgolf brings a highly entertaining, gamified element to winter practice. With fully climate-controlled bays, you can stay completely comfortable while hitting real golf balls out into the night sky.

While it might not provide the granular data of a TrackMan, it is absolutely perfect for target practice, working on your alignment, and keeping your golf muscles engaged. Plus, the automated ball tracking provides enough feedback to make the session highly productive.
