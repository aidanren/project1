Home | Next Location | Previous Location

Location: 7600 E 1st Pl, Denver

The Hangar Club offers a premium indoor golf experience utilizing state-of-the-art TrackMan simulator bays. It's a massive step up from a standard hitting net, providing incredibly accurate feedback on every metric of your swing.

Beyond just hitting the range, this facility allows you to play full, iconic courses digitally. It's a fantastic way to keep your course management skills sharp and challenge yourself with different lies and conditions when local courses are unplayable.
